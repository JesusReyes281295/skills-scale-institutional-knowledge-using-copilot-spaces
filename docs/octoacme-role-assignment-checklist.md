## 3. Role Confirmation Steps

- [ ] Each assigned person has been notified and has accepted the role
- [ ] Each assigned person has reviewed the relevant persona description in [Roles & Personas](octoacme-roles-and-personas.md)
- [ ] Responsibilities, decision rights, and escalation paths have been discussed at project kickoff
- [ ] Any role gaps (roles with no assignee) have been explicitly acknowledged, with a plan to cover the responsibilities (e.g., PM takes on BA duties for small projects)
- [ ] The confirmed role assignments are documented in the Project Charter or one-pager

---

## 4. Cross-Role Interaction Reminders

- **UX Designer ↔ Product Manager**: Align on user research findings and design direction before sprint planning
- **Business Analyst ↔ Project Manager**: Surface scope risks and open questions before planning begins
- **Release Manager ↔ QA / Testing**: Agree on release-readiness gates and smoke-test criteria at least one sprint before the target release
- **DevOps Engineer ↔ Release Manager**: Confirm pipeline readiness and rollback plan prior to each deployment
- **Support / Customer Success ↔ Product Manager**: Establish a cadence for sharing customer feedback that feeds into backlog prioritization
